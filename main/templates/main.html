Service

These are how you can use our service to help people and the environment

Open Donation

Users log in to create a new post for their used items. These posts can be viewed by other users.

Contact and Arrage for Pickup

Users who need a particular item may contact its donator and arrange a meeting to pick it up.

Close Donation

Donators close their donation and get points after it has been successfully handed over.

Call To Action

Our environment has been polluted by excessive amounts of waste that could have been reused or recycled. Let's reduce waste by donating it to those in need!
